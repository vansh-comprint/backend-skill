# LinkedIn

## Algorithm Signal Hierarchy ([DATE])

| Rank | Signal | Weight | Brand Strategy | How We Measure |
|------|--------|--------|---------------|----------------|
| 1 | **Dwell Time** | ~35% | Long-form text that rewards reading. White space, line breaks, storytelling. | LinkedIn Analytics → Impressions vs reads |
| 2 | **Meaningful Comments (10+ words)** | ~25% | End every post with a specific question. Not "thoughts?" — ask "What's your experience with X?" | LinkedIn Analytics → Comments |
| 3 | **Shares/Reposts** | ~20% | Contrarian takes and data-backed insights get shared most. Make people want to say "my network needs to see this." | LinkedIn Analytics → Reposts |
| 4 | **Reactions (beyond Like)** | ~15% | Insightful and Celebrate reactions signal quality. Funny and Like signal less. | LinkedIn Analytics → Reaction breakdown |
| 5 | **Profile clicks** | ~5% | Curiosity-driven hooks that make readers check who wrote this. | LinkedIn Analytics → Profile views |

## LinkedIn Engagement Protocol

|-----------|--------|-----|

## LinkedIn Algorithm Risks

| Risk | Why Dangerous | Mitigation |
|------|--------------|------------|
| External links in post body | LinkedIn suppresses posts with outbound links | Put links in FIRST COMMENT, not the post |
| Editing within 4 hours | Resets algorithm distribution | Write final copy before posting |
| Engagement pods | Detectable, results in reach penalty | Organic engagement only |
| Too many hashtags | >5 hashtags reduces distribution | Hard limit: 3-5 at end of post |
| Posting more than 1x/day | Posts cannibalize each other | Max 1 post per day, space 18+ hours apart |

# YouTube

## Algorithm Signal Hierarchy ([DATE])

| Rank | Signal | Weight | Brand Strategy | How We Measure |
|------|--------|--------|---------------|----------------|
| 1 | **Click-Through Rate (CTR)** | ~30% | Thumbnail + title optimization. Test 3 thumbnail variants mentally before choosing. | YouTube Studio → CTR |
| 2 | **Average View Duration (AVD)** | ~30% | Hook in first 30 sec, pattern interrupts every 60 sec, no fluff. | YouTube Studio → Audience retention |
| 3 | **Session Time** | ~20% | End screens, playlists, suggested video optimization. Keep viewers on YouTube. | YouTube Studio → Traffic sources |
| 4 | **Engagement (comments, likes, shares)** | ~15% | "Comment below" CTAs, community posts, polls. | YouTube Studio → Engagement |
| 5 | **Upload Consistency** | ~5% | Regular schedule signals reliability to algorithm. | Internal tracking |

## Current YouTube Rules

### Content Specs
| Format | Resolution | Aspect Ratio | Duration | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------------|----------|-------|
| Shorts | 1080 x 1920 | 9:16 | Under 60 sec | Similar to Reels. Hook in first 2 sec. |
| Long-form | 1920 x 1080 | 16:9 | 8-15 min optimal | Under 8 min misses mid-roll ads |
| Thumbnail | 1280 x 720 | 16:9 | N/A | Max 2MB. 3 elements max: face + text + context |
| Community Post | Text/image/poll | N/A | N/A | Good for engagement between uploads |

### Tags & SEO
- **Title:** 60 chars max, keyword-front-loaded
- **Description:** First 2 lines appear in search. Include keyword naturally.
- **Tags:** 5-10 relevant tags (mix broad + specific)
- **Chapters:** Add timestamps for videos > 3 min (improves retention + SEO)

## YouTube Algorithm Risks
