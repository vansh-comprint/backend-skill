## Promotion / Demotion Rules

### Promote: Watch → Active
- Account consistently produces content in our niche
- Follower count is within comparable range (0.1x to 10x our size)
- Active roster has an open slot (or candidate outperforms a current active)
- At least 2 weeks on Watch List with consistent posting

### Demote: Active → Watch
- Account goes inactive (no posts in 2+ weeks)
- Account pivots to a different niche
- Account grows beyond useful comparison range (>20x our size)
- A Watch List candidate is more strategically relevant

### Graduate (Remove Entirely)
- Account is permanently inactive or deleted
- Account has been on Watch for 4+ weeks with no actionable insights
- Niche divergence makes comparison meaningless
